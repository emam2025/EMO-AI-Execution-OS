## 1. Executive Summary

This document announces the official and successful closure of the current architectural cycle, marking the transition of **EMO AI** from an "Agent Framework" to a **Distributed AI Execution Operating System**. This transition was achieved through the establishment of integrated operational layers combining Cognitive Orchestration, Isolated Execution, Security Governance, and Comprehensive Audit, while maintaining **Default Deny** and **Human-in-the-Loop** principles across all sectors.

## 6. Strategic Roadmap

The immediate next step is **Pilot Expansion** before transitioning to Phase H (Computer Use Runtime), to collect real-world data on:

1. **Manufacturing Pilot:** Latency < 100ms, predictive maintenance reliability.
2. **Energy Pilot:** NERC-CIP compliance in grid monitoring environment.
3. **Water Pilot:** Safety Gates and human approval in distributed environment.
4. **Healthcare Simulation:** HIPAA compliance, unauthorized data export rejection rates.

*Note: Phase H (Browser/Desktop/Vision Automation) is deferred until current models prove stable in pilot environments.*
